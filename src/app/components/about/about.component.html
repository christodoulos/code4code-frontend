About

CODE4CODE is funded by the Research and Innovation Foundation of the Republic of Cyprus under the Call PRE_SEED/1221 of " CREATION AND INITIAL DEVELOPMENT OF STARTUPs WITH INTERNATIONAL ORIENTATION (Pre-Seed)" Program of RESTART 2016-2020. The project was launched on November 1st, 2022 and is expected to be completed by April 30th, 2024.

Title: A novel Adaptive Learning Platform for Python and Javascript, utilizing an AI-driven learning and content authoring domain structure

Summary:
Code4Code focuses on coding skills training and learning, and aims to bring teachers and learners in control of their knowledge and content-authoring environments. Teaching and learning how to write proper software code has been a challenge for tutors and learners at various educational levels (secondary and tertiary primarily) due to the limitations of available authoring platforms for coding to address the tailored needs of tutors and learners. In Code4Code project, we will design and develop the first holistic coding learning approach for Python and Javascript that utilizes artificial intelligence (AI) techniques. Information semantics, reasoning, and machine learning (ML) algorithms that learn from real-time interaction with the learning environment) will facilitate automatic decision-making feedback mechanisms in remote/self-learning setups. Code4Code introduces a novel Web platform that provides a Coding authoring subsystem for tutors and a self-learning environment for students, supported by an AI/ML module. The Code4Code Platform aims to reach beyond TRL5 by the end of the Project with validations and demonstrations organized in secondary and tertiary educational institutes in Cyprus.

Objectives:
Code4Code project mission is to:

  1. Develop a novel Web platform that will support teachers and learners by providing a dynamic and personalized gamified environment, adaptive to users' objectives and needs via artificial intelligence, a novel authoring tool, and a database with specific content in different curriculum levels.
  2. Validate the applicability and effectiveness of the Platform to be developed and its modules at secondary and tertiary education levels to support the viability of the new startup. To undertake several pilot applications necessary for the sufficient training of Code4Code machine learning models;
  3. Perform a thorough feasibility study, including market analysis and business planning. Determine the scheme of the integration flow of the Platform in the operation of several learning institutions. Undertake product promotion activities related to advertising and participation in conferences and exhibitions.
  4. Attract the interest of potential users of the Platform and policymakers. Offer free trial subscriptions to educational institutions upon targeted visits to present the product.
